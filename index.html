CrossFit

CrossFit emphasizes constantly varied high-intensity functional movements, encompassing calisthenics, weightlifting, plyometrics, bodyweight exercises, rowing, and more.

Boutique

A boutique gym is a small, intimate fitness facility with a focus on exclusivity with a community centric business model.

Bent-over row

A bent-over row is a weight training exercise that targets a variety of back muscles. Which ones are targeted varies on form. The bent over row is often used for both bodybuilding and powerlifting.
